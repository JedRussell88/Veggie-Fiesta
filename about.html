At Veggie Fiesta, we’re more than just a food truck  –  we’re a celebration of flavour, community, and local pride. Nestled in the heart of Nimbin, Australia, we serve vibrant, vegetarian street food inspired by the bold tastes of Latin America. Every dish is crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, bringing colour, spice, and authenticity straight to your plate. Miesha has called Nimbin home for most of his life and has always had a passion for creating delicious vegetarian meals. While Priscilla brings the rich culinary heritage of Latin America to every recipe, ensuring each bite tells a story. Together, we’re committed to supporting local businesses, celebrating our community, and sharing the joy of great food with everyone who finds their way to our truck. Follow the aromas, and join us for a feast your taste buds will never forget.
