Catering Now Available!
Veggie Fiesta is excited to explore catering for the very first time! Whether you're planning a party, a community gathering or want to provide a healthy sustainable vegeterian option, we have a wide selections of options to cater something delicious for your occasion.

If you'd like to learn more about what we can offer, please reach out through our Contact Us page  –  we'd love to chat about how we can cater your next event!

Tip: For private events please include how many people you expect as this will help us plan how much food to cater and will have an impact on your mandatory deposit requirement.

Note: Community events where there are other food options available will not require a deposit.
